\textbf{CDF of Epoch Dirty Proportion of Pages}

\noindent
\textit{Dirty proportion} of a page is the number of dirty cache blocks divided by the total number of cache blocks within the page. \textit{IPE} is the number of Instructions Per Epoch. \textit{DR}, or Dirty Rate, is the total number of dirty cache blocks in all pages during an epoch. In a figure, different curves correspond to different page sizes. ``k'', ``m'' and ``b'' stand for thousand(s), million(s) and billion(s), respectively.
